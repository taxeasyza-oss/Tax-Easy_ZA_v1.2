Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about TaxEasy ZA and South African tax.

Will I get a tax refund or do I owe SARS money?

This depends on how much tax was already deducted from your salary (PAYE) versus your actual tax liability. If more tax was deducted than you owe, you'll get a refund. If less was deducted, you'll owe SARS money. Our calculator shows you exactly which scenario applies to you.

How can I maximize my tax refund legally?

Key strategies include: maximizing retirement annuity contributions (up to 27.5% of income or R350,000), claiming all medical aid tax credits, deducting legitimate business expenses, claiming solar panel installations, and ensuring all allowable deductions are captured. Our calculator optimizes these for you.

What is TaxEasy ZA 2025?

TaxEasy ZA 2025 is a professional online tax calculator designed to help South African taxpayers estimate their tax liability or refund for the 2025 tax year (1 March 2024 - 28 February 2025). It uses official SARS tax tables and is 100% SARS compliant.

Is TaxEasy ZA 100% SARS compliant?

Yes, TaxEasy ZA 2025 uses the official SARS tax tables and rates from the Government Gazette. However, this provides estimates only - your actual tax liability may differ based on individual circumstances not captured in the calculator.

What information do I need to use the calculator?

You will need your personal details, income information (as per your IRP5), and details of any deductions or allowances you wish to claim. Having your IRP5 and other tax certificates handy will make the process quicker.

How accurate is the tax calculation?

Our calculator provides an accurate estimate based on the information you provide and the current SARS tax tables. However, it is an estimate and not a final assessment. For complex tax situations, we recommend consulting a registered tax practitioner.

What is an IRP5?

An IRP5 certificate is a tax certificate issued by your employer, detailing your income, deductions, and Pay As You Earn (PAYE) tax paid during the tax year. It is crucial for completing your annual tax return.

Can I generate a PDF report?

Yes, you can generate a basic PDF report for free, which summarizes your tax calculation. For a more detailed analysis, including tax planning recommendations and eFiling instructions, you can purchase a professional report.

How do discount codes work?

What is eFiling?

eFiling is SARS's online platform that allows taxpayers to submit their income tax returns electronically. Our professional report includes detailed instructions to guide you through the eFiling process.
