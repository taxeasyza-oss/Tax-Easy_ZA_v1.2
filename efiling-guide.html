eFiling Guide for 2025

A step-by-step guide to submitting your tax return on SARS eFiling.

Step 1: Log in to SARS eFiling

Go to the official SARS eFiling website: www.sarsefiling.co.za. Log in with your unique username and password. If you are a new user, you will need to register first.

Step 2: Navigate to Your Tax Return

Once logged in, go to the "Returns" tab and select "File a Return". Choose "Personal Income Tax (ITR12)" for the relevant tax year (2025).

Step 3: Complete the Tax Return Wizard

SARS will present a wizard to customize your tax return. Answer the questions accurately. This will determine which sections of the tax return you need to complete.

Step 4: Capture Your Income

Your IRP5/IT3(a) information may be pre-populated by SARS. Verify that this information is correct. If not pre-populated, you will need to capture the details from your IRP5 and other income-related tax certificates.

Step 5: Enter Your Deductions

Use the information from your TaxEasy ZA report to enter your deductions. This includes retirement annuity contributions, medical aid contributions, and other qualifying expenses. Ensure you have the necessary supporting documents (e.g., medical aid certificate, donation receipts).

Step 6: Review and Submit

Once you have completed all the required sections, SARS will provide a summary of your tax calculation. Review this carefully and compare it with your TaxEasy ZA report. If you are satisfied, submit the return.

Step 7: Make a Payment or Await Your Refund

If you owe SARS money, you can make a payment via EFT, credit card, or at a bank. If you are due a refund, it will be paid into your registered bank account. You can track the status of your return on the eFiling website.

Important Notes

  • Keep all your supporting documents for at least five years, as SARS may request them for verification.
  • If you need assistance, you can call the SARS Contact Centre on 0800 00 7277.
  • The deadline for non-provisional taxpayers is typically in October/November. Check the SARS website for the exact date.
